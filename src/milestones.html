One

One of our first milestones was a collaboration with renowned Dutch fashion photographer Otto van den Toorn. Ronald knew him from his earlier days creating brand videos in the fashion world.

We were given full creative freedom to design his new portfolio site — a project that was met with great enthusiasm in the design community and recognized by leading platforms like Awwwards, The FWA, and even won Site of the Year at the CSS Awards.

Two

After working together on a few freelance development projects, the connection with Rob quickly became clear. We shared the same passion, drive, and creative standards, collaboration felt natural from day one.

It wasn’t long before we asked Rob to officially join us. From there, we became a strong trio, each with our own discipline, perfectly complementing one another. That balance became the foundation for how we work to this day.

Three

Over the years, we’ve shared plenty of great moments outside of work too. We’ve always believed in balancing hard work with time to unwind and reconnect with nature.

We’re all fans of staying active — whether it’s mountain biking, swimming, working out, wakeboarding, or a relaxed SUP session. And of course, it often ends with a well-earned pint together.

We also love catching up over lunch in our beautiful hometown of Roermond, or escaping to a cozy cabin in the southern hills of the Netherlands — a place to reset, refocus our vision for the months ahead, and simply enjoy the peace and quiet of nature.
